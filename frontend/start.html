What is relays.social?

Relays is a new social network for sharing real moments, connecting with friends, and discovering stories. It's private, fun, and built for you—not advertisers.

  • Post stories, photos, and videos that expire
  • Chat with friends in real time
  • Privacy-first: you control your audience
  • Discover trending content and new people
  • Earn streaks and badges for activity
  • No ads, no tracking, no nonsense
